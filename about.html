My Story

I'm 6z7y. Back on Windows 7, my laptop was full of malware, ads, freezes, shutdowns, and overheating. That pushed me to focus on privacy and low resource use. Now I run Void Linux with DWM.

What I Enjoy

I enjoy Japanese and English music for language learning and as coding background my_music. I prefer clean, minimal setups so nothing distracts me.

What I Do

Spending most of my time alone at home has helped me focus on programming and languages. I don’t use social media — only Discord to stay in touch with people.

Programming Focus

Learning Rust for about 5 months, aiming to build small, efficient tools. Once I’m stronger in Rust, I plan to pick up C to explore low-level programming.

How I Keep Going

I’m convinced that pain is a big part of my personality, driving me toward change and keeping distance from others. Patience has been essential to stay on this path.

My Favorite Tools

  • st — simple and fast terminal emulator.
  • dmenu — lightweight and efficient application launcher.
  • tmux — terminal multiplexer for managing sessions.
  • fish-shell — user-friendly shell with autosuggestions.
  • nvim — modern, extensible text editor.
  • yt-dlp — powerful tool for downloading videos.
  • mpv — lightweight media player.
  • fzf — command-line fuzzy finder.
  • floorp — privacy-focused web browser.
