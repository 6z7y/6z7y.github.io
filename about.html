My Story

When I used Windows 7, my laptop was constantly plagued by malware, ads, freezes, unexpected shutdowns, and overheating. These frustrations led me to prioritize privacy and efficient resource usage. I now use Void Linux with DWM.

What I Enjoy

I enjoy Japanese and English music for language learning and as background while coding. Browse my some music. I prefer clean, minimal setups to maintain focus and avoid distractions.

What I Do

Spending most of my time working from home has helped me concentrate on programming and language learning. I avoid social media platforms—using only Discord to stay connected with others.

Programming Focus

I've been learning Rust for about five months, with the goal of building small, efficient tools. After improving my Rust skills, I plan to learn C to explore low-level programming concepts.

How I Keep Going

I believe challenges have shaped my personality, motivating me to pursue change and maintain independence. Patience has been essential for staying committed to this path.
